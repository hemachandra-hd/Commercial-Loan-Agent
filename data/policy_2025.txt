ACME COMMERCIAL BANKING - CREDIT RISK POLICY 2025
Version: 2.1 | Effective Date: Jan 1, 2025

SECTION 1: INDUSTRY RESTRICTIONS
The bank is strictly prohibited from lending to the following high-risk sectors:
1. Adult Entertainment
2. Cryptocurrency Exchanges or Mining Operations
3. Commercial Gambling / Casinos
4. Speculative Real Estate Development (without 50% pre-leasing)

SECTION 2: FINANCIAL COVENANTS & THRESHOLDS
To qualify for a standard commercial loan, the borrower must meet these minimums:
- Minimum Debt-Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR): 1.25x
- Minimum Credit Score (FICO SBSS): 680
- Maximum Debt-to-Equity Ratio: 4:1
- Minimum Time in Business: 2 Years

SECTION 3: LOAN AUTHORITY LIMITS
- Loan Officer Limit: Up to $1,000,000
- Senior Credit Officer: Up to $5,000,000
- Executive Risk Committee: Required for all loans exceeding $5,000,000

SECTION 4: COLLATERAL REQUIREMENTS
- Equipment Loans: LTV up to 80%
- Commercial Real Estate: LTV up to 75%
- Unsecured Loans: Capped at $100,000 per borrower.
